Ajeromi-Ifelodun Local Government

“Heartbeat of Ajegunle Sound & Resilience”

History

Carved out in 1996 from the old Lagos Metropolitan Area, this densely populated LGA has long been known as a working-class district with rich cultural traditions

Known For

Tincan Port Proximity

Major shipping and warehouse zones

Ajegunle Music Scene

Birthplace of Galala, Ajegunle sound, and many top artists

Cultural Diversity

High population density and vibrant mix of ethnicities

Location

Bordered by Apapa, Amuwo-Odofin, and Ifelodun LCDA
