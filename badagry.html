Badagry Local Government

“Where History, Heritage & Hope Meet”

History

Badagry, established centuries ago, was a key slave trade route and one of the first contact points between Europe and Nigeria. It played a critical role in Nigeria’s Christian and educational history

Known For

Slave Route & Relics

Home to the Point of No Return and slave museums chronicling the transatlantic slave trade

First Storey Building

Built in 1845 by Christian missionaries — the oldest of its kind in Nigeria

Tourism & Beaches

Features Coconut Beach, serene waterfronts, and cultural festivals

Location

Located at the western edge of Lagos State, bordering Ojo and Seme in Benin Republic
