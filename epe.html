Epe Local Government

“Fishing Roots and Quiet Coastal Charm”

History

Founded by the Ijebu people, Epe has deep fishing and trading roots and was once the base of King Kosoko during his exile from Lagos in the 1850s. It is historically tied to Yoruba monarchy and traditions.

Known For

Fish Market

A major fish processing and trading hub

Ebi Festival

Traditional event celebrating culture and unity

Agriculture

Key zone for Lagos food supply and cassava production

Location

Situated in the northeastern part of Lagos, bordered by Ibeju-Lekki and Ogun State.
