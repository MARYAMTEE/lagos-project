Ibeju-Lekki Local Government

“Lagos’s New Economic Frontier”

History

Carved from Eti-Osa in the early 1990s, Ibeju-Lekki transitioned from a quiet fishing district to a booming industrial and development axis

Known For

Lekki Deep Sea Port & Free Zone

Strategic corridor for shipping, oil, and logistics

Dangote Refinery

Africa’s largest oil refinery, drawing major investments

Eleko & Lakowe Beaches

Fast-growing coastal resorts and leisure destinations

Location

Southeastern Lagos, bordering Epe, Eti-Osa, and facing the Atlantic
