Surulere Local Government

"Heart of Lagos Culture & Sports"

History

Surulere was carved out in 1891, originally part of the old Eti-Osa, and Victoria Island & Lagos Metropolis. Developed as a residential estate and cultural hub, birthplace of Afrobeat and Nigerian cinema.

Known For

Stadium

Major hub for sports events

Culture

Culture & Theatre roots

Neighborhoods

Historic estates & creative hubs

Location

Surulere is bordered by Yaba, Mushin, and Lagos Mainland.
