Mushin Local Government

“Working-Class Engine of Hustle & Trade”

History

Mushin developed as a residential extension of colonial Lagos and became known for its entrepreneurial spirit and dense urban lifestyle

Known For

Textile & Fabric Markets

Major hub for lace, Ankara, and tailoring accessories in Nigeria

Mechanic & Auto Parts Hubs

Hosts large spare parts markets and car workshops

Cultural Vibes

Birthplace of many fuji and afrobeat musicians, contributing to Lagos’s street culture

Location

Central Lagos, bordered by Surulere, Oshodi-Isolo, and Lagos Mainland
