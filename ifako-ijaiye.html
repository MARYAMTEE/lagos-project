Ifako-Ijaiye Local Government

“Transit Hub of Growth and Family Living”

History

Created in 1996 from Agege LGA, Ifako-Ijaiye quickly grew into a suburban area with expanding residential estates and improved connectivity to central Lagos

Known For

Housing Estates

Harmony Estate, Iju Ishaga, and Fagba

Business & Tech Ojokoro Transport Links

Growing bus terminals and road access

Cultural Events

Known for community-centered festivals and local leaders

Location

Located in the western part of Lagos, bordered by Agege, Alimosho, and Ogun State
