Ikorodu Local Government

“Craft City on the Lagoon”

History

One of the oldest towns in Lagos State, Ikorodu was founded by settlers from Ijebu and Remo. It evolved from a rural town into a key suburban-industrial district

Known For

Ferry Terminal

Offers direct waterway access to Lagos Island, easing urban transit

Artisans & Local Industry

Skilled trades in woodwork, dyeing, and pottery define its economy

Traditional Festivals

Hosts annual Eluku and Oro festivals, preserving rich Yoruba traditions

Location

Located in the northeastern part of Lagos, bordered by Kosofe, Epe, and Ogun State
