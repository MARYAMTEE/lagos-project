Shomolu Local Government

“Print Capital and Creative Core”

History

Originally part of Lagos Mainland, Shomolu was officially designated an LGA in 1991. It quickly became Nigeria’s center for printing and publishing

Known For

Printing Industry

Supplies printed materials for publishers and campaign outfits across Nigeria

Youthful Arts Scene

Known for its music studios, design outfits, and local arts culture

Compact Residential Zones

Characterized by high-density housing and vibrant street life

Location

Nestled between Kosofe, Lagos Mainland, and Bariga LCDA
